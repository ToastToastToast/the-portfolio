Description

Tale of the Dragon's Mirage is a 2d blaster game for the Global Game Jam 2025. With 3 other group members, RyanEAX, Solarmoth, and Dancloud, the group was challenged with completing a game in only 2 days. Game Jam site hosted by San Jose State Uni. For this game, I created the background in the picture above.
Spring 2025


Story & Background

The jealous snake demon is trying to control the dragon’s territory by impersonating it and polluting its rivers. The dragon must reclaim its honor by using its bubbles to reveal the snake's plot and protect its home. In celebration of Chinese New Years 2025 - the Year of the Snake!
