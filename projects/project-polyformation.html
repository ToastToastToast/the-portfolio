Polyformation


Description

Polyformation is a 2d space game. The libraries used in this project are p5play, p5.sound, and scenemanager. Sounds and real life imagery are from pixabay and ArgoFox Youtube Channel.
Known bug: the game won't reset if you play the game again, requiring a refresh.


Story & Background

The narrative of the game is aliens are trying to steal the 3rd dimension, transforming the world into a 2d landscape so they can rebuild the world for themselves. The goal is to eliminate the aliens and return the world back to its original state. The narrative plays on the idea of skeumorphism and Flat Design graphics by incoporating these concepts into the narrative of the story.
