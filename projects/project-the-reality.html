The Reality


Description

This Blender project was for an SJSU Blender class Final. This class I learned basic rigging, sculpting, and adobe substance painter.
Created: Fall 2023


Story & Background

"The Reality" is about a metallic humanoid ram that wishes to coexist with nature and is happily chasing a butterfly. However, the flames of darkness have found the ram, reminding that they are both the same in which they inevitably destroy the environment. This is a reflection of how people may say they wish to do something but don't realize the reality they live in requires them to go against what they say. For example, in modern times, people may say they support global climate change yet still continue to not be effecient with house appliance usage.
