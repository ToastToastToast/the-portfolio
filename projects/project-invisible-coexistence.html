Alone


Description

A rendered image of stuffed animals sitting at the park. This image uses photogrammetry to place realistic 3D models into a scene. The goal is to evoke the thought of other worlds existing alongside our daily lives. These worlds are sometimes make-believe, sometimes real yet people turn a blind eye to the world's existence.
