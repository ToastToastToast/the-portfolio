A Place to Call Home


Description

A Place To Call Home is a 3js open world. Models are created in blender. Controls to traverse through the world are "WASD" for movement and "J" and "L" to look left and right. Collect coins that spawn every 10 seconds in hopes of buying a house!
Created: Fall 2024


Artist Statement

A Place To Call Home is about the skyrocketing prices of housing, resulting in difficulty for generation Z to ever obtain a home. Only two decades earlier, housing was both available and affordable for new families. There's also lots of people who are homeless in America because of the competitive job market and unaffordable housing prices. The 3js world is a virtual experience of the very problem that society faces.
