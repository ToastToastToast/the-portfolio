Ryan Chong

3D Artist & Game Developer

Ryan Chong is a Digital Media Artist focusing towards the mediums of game development and 3D Modeling. Playing countless hours of different games during childhood, Ryan's goal is to develop monumental interactive and emotional video games ranging from 2d pixel platformers to 3D immersive experiences. Ryan's work blends the imagination with reality, breathing the presence of life into the world's surroundings. Completed a Bachelors of Fine Arts from San Jose State University.
